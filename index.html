Why this analysis is needed?

Our target audience for the developed system is the Ottawa police. The analysis on the rate of bike thefts around Ottawa would help them to identify areas which require stricter monitoring around prone areas. This cool can be accessible by locals in Ottawa to get information on areas which are susceptible to thefts which can help them decide when to visit the area or the necessary precautions to be taken etc. The system designed will help our stakeholders to take necessary actions for their respective purposes.
